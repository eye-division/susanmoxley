Your privacy, and your experience of using this site, is important to me. 

This website functions as on-line Portfolio for Jo Broad and KindleCo. It additionally offers insights, links published resources, accessible through it. These are available to read or download for free.

The site is not used to transact any goods or services directly. 

This site uses cookies to help us continue to improve visitor experience. If you use this site, we’ll assume you’re OK with this.

If you use our online contact form via this website, we will store the information you supply for two purposes:

a) to respond to your enquiy, where appropriate

b) to keep in touch with you, for example through occasional emails, newsletters and notifications which may interest you, in line with your enquiry (e.g. when a new resource is published) 

As a very small enterprise, every effort is made to keep your data secure and it will never be knowingly passed to a third party without your explicit permission.

You can contact us, via this website, at any time to ask to have your details removed from our database.
