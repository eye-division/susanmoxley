Susan has worked as a printmaker since studying printmaking in the 70’s.

            She has developed from a traditional printmaker into an artist using unconventional methods                          to produce ‘Graphic Paintings’ that take advantage of multi layering and graphic marks

that result in free and painterly works.

Her subjects, while often appear playful are usually topical and often political.
