Susan Moxley's prints and paintings are exhibited extensively in the UK and internationally, where they take their place in both public and private collections.

The diversity of her work including fine art, children's books, novel covers and stained glass demonstrates her curiosity and passion for creativity in all forms.

Susan's work is always immediately recognizable through it's free flowing,painterly lines and exuberant colours.
