Susan Moxley's PRINTS and PAINTINGS are exhibited extensively in the UK and internationally, where they take their place in both public and private collections.

The diversity of her work including fine art, children's books, novel covers and stained glass demonstrating her curiosity and passion for creativity in all forms. Over the last few years she has designed and made SILVER JEWELLERY which usually incorporated found stones. Her pieces  reference ancient classic forms, which relate closely to her 2 dimensional work.

Susan's work is always immediately recognisable through it's free flowing, painterly lines.

While they appear playful, they often carry a topical and contemporary message.
