Susan Moxley's prints and paintings are exhibited extensively in the UK and internationally, where they take their place in both public and private collections.

The diversity of her work including fine art, ceramics, textiles and stained glass demonstrating her curiosity and passion for creativity in all forms. Over the last few years she has designed and made Silver Jewellery. Her pieces  reference ancient classic forms, which relate closely to her 2 dimensional work.

She is showing her textiles and ceramics with Jenny Blyth Fine art on StandE7 at COLLECT The crafts council Art Fair at Somerset House in London on 28th February- 2nd March
