PERSONAL
Born and brought up in South Africa.
Married to Children's book illustrator, Korky Paul.
Two children Zoë and Oska.
Lives in Oxford, England.

EDUCATION
Studied Fine Art at Natal College of Art, Durban, South Africa.
Qualified  with Highest results in Republic of South  Africadistinction andin Printmaking and Illustration.
Postgraduate study in Printmaking at Croydon College
of Art U.K.

CURRENT ACTIVITIES
Works as Printmaker, Painter, Illustrator and stained glass artist.
Currently exhibiting with Four  Impressions, a 'gallery' formed by Susan Moxley, Clare Bassett and Jessie Lee to exhibit at  Art Fairs  around the country.
