PERSONAL

Born and brought up in South Africa.
Married to Children's book illustrator, Korky Paul.
Two children Zoë and Oska.
Lives in Oxford, England.

EDUCATION

Studied Fine Art at Natal College of Art, Durban, South Africa.
Qualified  with Highest results in Republic of South  Africa
distinction andin Printmaking and Illustration.
P ostgraduate study in Printmaking at Croydon College
of Art U.K.

CURRENT ACTIVITIES

Works as Printmaker, Painter, Illustrator and stained glass artist.
Currently exhibiting with  4Impressions gallery formed by Susan Moxley, Clare Bassett and Jessie Lee to exhibit at  Art Fairs  around the country.
